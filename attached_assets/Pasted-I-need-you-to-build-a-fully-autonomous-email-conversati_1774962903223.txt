**PART 3 — RESEND INBOUND WEBHOOK**

Create a new POST route at `/api/outreach/inbound-reply`.

Resend will send inbound emails to this endpoint. The payload from Resend includes:
- `from` — the prospect's email address
- `subject` — the reply subject
- `text` — the plain text body of their reply

When this webhook fires:

1. Find the matching lead in `outreach_leads` by matching `from` email to the lead's email field
2. If no match found, log it and return 200
3. If `auto_reply_enabled` is false on this lead, log it and return 200 — do not reply
4. If `awaiting_handoff` is true on this lead, send me a notification email and return 200 — do not auto reply
5. Append their reply to `conversation_thread` as `role: 'prospect'`
6. Call the AI reply handler (see Part 4)
7. Send the AI reply via Resend
8. Append the AI reply to `conversation_thread` as `role: 'chris'`
9. Update `pipeline_status` with whatever the AI returns
10. If the AI returns `handoff: true`, set `awaiting_handoff = true` and `auto_reply_enabled = false` on the lead, then send me a notification email at chris@blackridgeplatforms.com
